Features

Why SkillBridge?

Everything you need to learn Anthropic Academy in your native language.

🌐
Real-time Translation
Translate entire course pages into your language with one click. Supports 31 languages including CJK, Arabic, Hindi, and more.
🤖
AI Learning Tutor
Ask questions about course material in your language. Get explanations, summaries, and help understanding complex AI topics.
📚
Protected Terms
Technical terms like "prompt", "token", and "fine-tuning" stay in English so you learn the correct vocabulary used in the industry.
🎥
Video Subtitles
YouTube course videos get translated subtitles automatically. Learn visually in your own language.
💚
100% Free
Powered by Gemini 2.0 Flash + Claude Sonnet 4 via Puter.js. No API keys, no sign-up, no subscription. Just install and learn.
🔒
Copyright-Safe & Private
Translates on-the-fly in your browser only. Never stores, caches, or redistributes original course content. Your data stays local.

Getting Started

How It Works

From install to learning in under a minute.

1
Install
Add SkillBridge from the Chrome Web Store. One click, no config.
2
Visit Anthropic Academy
Go to anthropic.skilljar.com and open any course.
3
Select Language
Click the globe icon and pick your preferred language.
4
Learn!
Read translated content and chat with the AI tutor in your language.
